MARCO'S WORK

Preventing Gun Violence and Improving School Safety

In response to the horrific shooting in Parkland, Florida, Marco unveiled a comprehensive plan to prevent gun violence and keep weapons out of the hands of people who should not have them.

Leading With Ivanka Trump On Paid Family Leave

During the tax reform debate, Marco fought to ensure working parents benefitted by including an expansion of the child tax credit. Now, he’s setting his sights on another pro-family, pro-worker policy: paid family leave.

Delivering Disaster Relief Funds for Florida

In the wake of Hurricane Irma, Marco and his staff sprung into action by spearheading Disaster Recovery Assistance Centers across Florida.

Delivering Disaster Relief Funds for Puerto Rico

Immediately after Hurricane Maria, Marco visited Puerto Rico to survey the destruction and receive a briefing on recovery efforts at the emergency operations center in San Juan.

Securing Expansion Of The Child Tax Credit

Marco believes we cannot have a strong nation without strong families, which is why, since 2014, he has led the movement for an enhanced child tax credit to help hard-working American families.

Passage Of RACE For Children Act

Marco was a lead sponsor of bipartisan legislation to increase cancer treatment options for children, which was signed into law by President Trump in August of 2017.

Rolling Back Pres. Obama's Misguided Cuba Policy

As a Cuban-American whose parents fled Fidel Castro’s communist dictatorship and Chairman of the Foreign Relations Committee’s Subcommittee on the Western Hemisphere, Marco is the Senate’s most influential voice on America’s Cuba policy.

Toughening Sanctions On Maduro & His Cronies

From his position as Chairman of the Foreign Relations Committee’s Subcommittee on the Western Hemisphere, Marco has been the chief critic of the criminal government in Venezuela and the leading advocate for Venezuelan people.

Championing Human Rights

Marco is a leading voice in the Senate championing human rights across the globe and has been a vocal advocate for combatting human trafficking both domestically and abroad.

VA Accountability And Reform Bill

Marco’s legislation to increase accountability at the Veterans Administration was signed into law by President Trump in June.

Hurricane Irma Disaster Assistance

In the wake of Hurricane Irma, Marco and his staff sprung into action by spearheading Disaster Recovery Assistance Centers across Florida.

Strengthening NASA

Florida is home to America’s space program, and Marco worked with Senate colleagues to reintroduce and push for the NASA Transition Authorization Act, which was into law by the president in March.

Looking Out For Florida's Fishing Industry

Fishing is a major industry in Florida, and Marco looked out for our state’s fishermen by pushing federal agencies to allow for an expanded recreational red snapper season.

Securing New Aircraft For Tyndall Air Force Base

In January 2017, Marco urged then-acting Secretary of the Air Force Deborah James to select one of the Florida military installations as the home base for the MQ-9 Reaper UAS, a next-generation unmanned aerial vehicle capable of acting as a “hunter-killer."
