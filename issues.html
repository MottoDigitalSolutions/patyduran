American Global Leadership

Marco anchors his national security and foreign policy views in the principles of American military strength, protection of our economy in a globalized world, and proud advocacy for our core values. The world is at its safest when America is at its strongest.

Florida First

Marco is a leader on national and international issues but always puts his duty to Floridians first. His Senate staff is spread across Washington D.C., and eight Florida cities, and is dedicated to serving all of his constituents.

Preparing Americans For The New Economy

Since joining the Senate, Marco has worked tirelessly to help Florida businesses compete in the 21st-century economy. Marco knows that cutting taxes and reducing regulations is key to assisting Florida businesses—especially our 2.3 million small businesses...

Protecting the Family

Marco believes the family, not the government, is the most important unit in American society. Families are how we pass down our values to the next generation, and if the family breaks down, society breaks down.
